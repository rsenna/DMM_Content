Chapter 23 - Composing and Designing Bass

The drums and bass form the backbone of electronic dance music. In fact, there have been many records that consist of little more than the drums, bass and a vocal (i.e., most Tech House and Techno). Here, we'll look at the general music and sound design for creating bass lines.

Sine Waves played an interval apart

In the above example, two sine waves are playing an interval apart. Note how the phase cancelation creates an interesting modulated tone.
