Chapter 27 - House Music

The divergence of House music over the past 20 years has resulted in a genre that has become hopelessly fragmented and it can be difficult to identify what constitutes a house record today. It can range from funky (a disco vibe) through to vocal or deep, drawing inspiration from Techno (Tech House).
