Chapter 11 - Compressors and Compression

Although originally compression was employed in a way that was not noticeable to the ear, the sound of evident compression has become a fundamental element of electronic dance music. For this chapter, we'll examine a couple of typical compression effects - side-chaining a mix to make it pump energetically and side-chaining a bass to allow room for a kick drum.
